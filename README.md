## The assignment in one line

Write a newsletter for founder-led Indian mid-market companies (₹50Cr–₹500Cr) using a real-world case study to argue: you cannot grow without a dedicated improvement team and a defined protocol — structural fixes outperform motivation.

---

## Case study used

**Jacobs Manufacturing Company ("Jake Brake"), Bloomfield, Connecticut — 1985–1988.**
The origin story of the Danaher Business System. A struggling diesel truck brake manufacturer turned around through structural separation of improvement from operations — becoming the model Danaher would apply across every acquisition for four decades.
